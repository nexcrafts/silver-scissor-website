About Silver Scissor

A grooming studio built on one idea — consistency, at a fair price, every single visit.

✂

Silver Scissor Hair Studio sits in the heart of P.E.C.H.S., Karachi — a grooming and wellness studio for men, with services delivered by a team of skilled male and female staff.

We built our menu around one simple frustration: most grooming visits come with unclear pricing and inconsistent results. Every service we offer, from a quick haircut to a full body massage, is listed with its exact rate — by staff member — so there's never a surprise at the chair.

Whether you're in for a 15-minute trim or a full relaxation session, our goal is the same: precise, professional work, on your schedule, without the guesswork.

✂
